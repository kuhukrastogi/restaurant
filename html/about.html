ABOUT

Virasat, as the name inspires a legacy, works towards preserving it for the generations to come and be aware about its enriching history. Known for its uniqueness, this only heritage restaurant of the city gives you a regal treat. this is a lavishly decorated space serving extravagant multi-course thalis. Best experience!! Excellent service and at par authentic Rajasthani Cuisine. Guests sit on the floor and enjoy multi-course thalis. The entire character of the place is conceptualised keeping in mind the rich heritage, enthralling culture, enticing customs and an amazing array of delicious cuisine of Rajasthan. Indulge your taste buds with delicious cuisine and live up the culture and tradition, magnificent art and artefacts, and the brilliant past of this culturally significant state. Believed to be the most authentic recreation of historical dining and opulent lifestyle of the royal pedigree, Virasat becomes a must visit for everyone who lands in the Heritage City of Rajasthan.
